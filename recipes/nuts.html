Grandma Marcilha's Glazed Roasted Nuts


Picture by Google

Ingredients

  • 250g of the nuts of your choice: almonds/walnuts/nuts/hazelnuts
    - you can mixed them all if you want to 😉
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 coffee spoon of cinnamon powder
  • 1 teaspoon of powdered chocolate

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a cooking pot.
    -It is highly recommended that you use a non-stick pan.
  2. Over medium heat, stir the entire mixture until all the water has evaporated.
    - When it starts to crumble, turn off the heat.
  3. Place everything on a non-stick baking sheet.
    -If you don't have one, line a baking sheet with aluminum foil)
  4. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees and bake for 20 minutes.
