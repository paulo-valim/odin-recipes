Creamy Delight Cream Cheese


Picture by Google

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of milk 3,2% (or higher)
  • 4 tablespoons of alcohol vinegar
  • 2 heaping tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 250 ml additional milk
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Boil the milk until it rises.
  2. After boiling, turn off the heat and add 4 tablespoons of vinegar.
  3. Stir until all the milk curdles.
  4. Strain the mixture retaining the ricotta.
  5. Place the ricotta in a blender along with the butter and milk and blend well.
  6. Put in the fridge for a few hours
